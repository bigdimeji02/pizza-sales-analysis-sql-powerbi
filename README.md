# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis (SQL + Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes pizza sales data to understand business performance, customer demand patterns, and product performance.  

The goal is to uncover insights that can help improve decision-making in areas such as inventory planning, staffing, and product strategy.

## 🔍 Key Business Questions

- When are the busiest sales periods?
- Which pizza categories generate the most revenue?
- Which products perform best and worst?
- How does customer ordering behavior vary over time?

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Peak sales occur between **12–1 PM and 6 PM**, indicating strong lunch and dinner demand  
- **Classic pizzas** contribute the highest share of total revenue  
- Revenue trends show stronger performance toward the **end of the week (weekends)**  
- Some pizzas consistently underperform, highlighting opportunities for **menu optimization**  
- Average Order Value (AOV) varies across days, suggesting differences in customer purchasing behavior
